## 9. FAQ

[Accordion-style. 4-6 questions max.]

**Do I need technical skills to set it up?**
No. If you can write an email, you can set up Angel Maria. Connect your Shopify store, add your email domain, and write your rules in plain language.

**Will customers know it's AI?**
No. Emails are sent from your own domain (support@yourstore.com) with your name and tone. It reads like a human wrote it.

**What happens when Angel Maria can't handle an email?**
It escalates to you. You get a notification, see the full thread, and can approve, edit, or write your own reply. You stay in control.

**Can I customize how it handles returns?**
Yes. You write the rules: what discount to offer first, when to escalate, what to never do. In plain language -- no code.

**What if I go over 1,000 emails?**
We'll let you know when you're getting close. Contact us and we'll sort out a plan that fits your volume.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes. We don't use your customer data to train AI models. Your data stays yours. Emails are processed securely and stored encrypted.

## Design Notes for Development

**Tone**: Direct, confident, no corporate fluff. Speak like a smart friend who runs a store. Avoid jargon. Every sentence should either solve a problem or prove value.

**Visual style**: Clean, modern, lots of whitespace. Think Linear or Vercel -- not Gorgias. This tool is simple and the design should reflect that.

**Mobile**: Hero, How It Works, and Pricing must work perfectly on mobile. Drop shippers check everything on their phone.

**Performance**: This page should load in under 2 seconds. No heavy animations. One font. Minimal images.

**SEO keywords to target**: dropshipping customer service, automated email support shopify, AI customer service dropshipping, return prevention tool, shopify support automation.
